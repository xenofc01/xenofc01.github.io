About

The Philippine Women's University-CDCEC Sta. Cruz is an extension campus of PWU. The institution offers quality education to students in the Southern Luzon region, particularly inside Laguna. Located exactly in the municipality of Sta. Cruz, province of Laguna, this branch of the aforementioned institution offers accessible and quality academic programs that aim to provide the needs of the local community. The main objective of PWU-Sta. Cruz is to give rise to graduates that are fully equipped with knowledge, skills, and values that are vital to succeed in their chosen fields that they decided to pursue, and most importantly, to make a positive impact in society.

Mission and Vision

Mission

To provide quality education that empowers individuals to achieve their full potential and contribute positively to society.

Vision

To be a globally recognized institution of learning, fostering innovation, leadership, and excellence in education.

Admission Requirements

Senior High School

  • 1” x 1” ID picture with Maroon or Red background (4 pcs.)
  • NSO/PSA Birth Certificate (Photocopy)
  • Long Brown Envelope (2 pcs.)
  • Original and two (2) Photocopies of the following:
    • Certificate of Good Moral Character
    • Form-138
    • Form-137
    • ESC Voucher, if any (Student from Private Schools)

College

  • 1” x 1” ID picture with Maroon or Red background (4 pcs.)
  • NSO/PSA Birth Certificate (2 photocopies)
  • Long Brown Envelope (2 pcs.)
  • Original and two (2) Photocopies of the following:
    • Certificate of Good Moral Character
    • Form-138 Senior High School Card
    • Form-137 Senior High School Permanent Record

For Transfer Students

  • Transfer Credential Certificate
  • Copy of Grades
  • Transcript of Records with Remarks: “Copy for PWU”

Strands

ABM Strand

The Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) Strand focuses on preparing students for business-related courses in college. It equips them with skills in accounting, management, and entrepreneurship.

Senior High School Offered Courses:

  • Business Administration
  • Accountancy
  • Marketing Management

College Offered Courses:

  • Entrepreneurship
  • Financial Management
  • Human Resource Management

STEM Strand

The Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) Strand is designed for students who are inclined toward science and mathematics. It prepares them for courses in engineering, medicine, and other technical fields.

Senior High School Offered Courses:

  • Engineering
  • Medicine
  • Information Technology

College Offered Courses:

  • Computer Science
  • Biology
  • Civil Engineering

HUMSS Strand

The Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) Strand is for students interested in social sciences, literature, and education. It develops critical thinking and communication skills.

Senior High School Offered Courses:

  • Psychology
  • Education
  • Political Science

College Offered Courses:

  • Mass Communication
  • Philosophy
  • Social Work

GAS Strand

The General Academic Strand (GAS) is for students who are undecided about their future career paths. It provides a balanced mix of subjects from other strands.

Senior High School Offered Courses:

  • General Studies
  • Interdisciplinary Studies
  • Customizable Electives

College Offered Courses:

  • General Education
  • Interdisciplinary Studies
  • Customizable Programs

TVL Strand

The Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Strand offers practical skills and training for students who want to pursue technical or vocational careers after high school.

Senior High School Offered Courses:

  • Automotive Servicing
  • Cookery
  • Electrical Installation and Maintenance

College Offered Courses:

  • Hotel and Restaurant Management
  • Tourism Management
  • Industrial Technology
